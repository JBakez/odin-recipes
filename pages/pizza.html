American Style Pizza

This pizza dish is a classic, enjoyed by millions of Americans year after year! With a nice thick, chewy, rising crust, plenty of toppings, and a delicious fresh sauce, this pizza recipe will have you coming back over and over again!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 181 grams fresh water
  • 278 grams bread flour

Recipe

  1. Heat fresh water to 100-110 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a mixing bowl, pour your water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes to bloom yeast.
  3. Once yeast is frothy, pour your flour in and begin to mix by hand or with a tool of choice.
  4. After a minute or two, your dough should be a nice shaggy ball of dough. It's okay if there are some pieces that won't stick yet.
  5. Add the oil and salt and begin to knead the dough inside the bowl with your hand.
  6. After 2-3 minutes, take your ball of dough to the counter and knead it for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  7. You'll know your dough is good when you can poke it and it bounces back!
  8. Form a doughball and place the ball into an oiled bowl. Let rest for 2-6 hours at room temperature. Make sure your cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid so the dough doesn't dry out.
  9. After several hours, you dough should have risen. You can now punch down the dough to release the gasses.
  10. This dough makes 2 medium pizzas - take your dough and separate.
  11. Shape your pizza dough and top with whatever you like!
