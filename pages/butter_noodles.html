Lemon Chicken w/ Butter Noodles

My butter noodles topped with lemony chicken is the perfect addition to your dinner lineup! Lots of creamy butter, fresh herbs, and perfectly cooked chicken with a twist of lemon. This recipe will impress anyone you are cooking for!

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs high quality butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 6 oz of noodle of choice (I use angel hair!)
  • 3/4 reserved pasta water
  • 6-8 oz of chicken breast
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Chili powder
  • 1 lemon

Recipe

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. While that is coming to heat, pound your chicken breast out thin and season to taste with garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, and salt.
  3. Place your chicken in an air fryer preheated to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Cook for 8-10 minutes until chicken has reached an internal temp of 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Chop your parsley and set aside.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, put the 4 tbs of butter in and zest your lemon over the butter
  6. Place your noodles in the water and cook according to package instructions.
  7. Remove chicken from air fryer, let rest for 5 minutes, and slice into thin pieces.
  8. Remove 3/4 cup of pasta water from cooking noodles and drain the noodles.
  9. Dump the noodles into the large bowl with the butter and stir until butter is melted.
  10. Add parsley.
  11. While stirring, add the pasta water slowly to emulsify the butter into a creamy sauce. You may not have to use all the water.
  12. When the butter noodles have a creamy texture, plate and top with sliced chicken.
  13. Squeeze lemon juice over the chicken and serve.
